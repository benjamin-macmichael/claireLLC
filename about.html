Claire Nickell

I am a Marriage and Family Therapy Associate and I have a passion for helping individuals and couples take control of their relationships and experience peace and clarity in their lives. I graduated from the University of Kentucky Marriage and Family Therapy program. During my time there I experienced success in helping individuals, couples and families heal from hurt and misunderstanding, and engage in new ways that brought joy and vitality back into their relationships.

Method of Therapy
I engage with individuals and couples who are looking for ways to improve and enrich their lives and help them do so on their own terms, utilizing personal strengths and motivations. Rather than taking an isolated psychological approach, I help individuals and families holistically, and we examine together all aspects of their lives and how their relationships and external life factors are impacting them. I build on wherever the client is at and we explore new pathways together; I take a client-led, therapist-augmented approach, and add my insights and direction as we go. I ask questions and explore with my clients what may be beneath the surface and difficult for them to identify on their own. I am influenced by an Emotionally Focused Therapy framework as well as a Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and solution-focused approach.
