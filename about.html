Claire Nickell M.S. Marriage and Family Therapy
I'm a Marriage and Family Therapy Associate with a passion for empowering individuals and couples to regain control of their relationships and find peace and clarity in their lives. I grew up in Nova Scotia, Canada, and have always felt it to be my life purpose to help others thrive. I graduated from the Marriage and Family Therapy program at the University of Kentucky, where I successfully assisted individuals, couples, and families in healing from trauma, hurt and misunderstandings. Together, we reclaimed the joy and vitality in their relationships. I want to help you to do the same.



Method of Therapy
I work with individuals and families seeking relational and/or personal therapy. Instead of solely focusing on psychology, I take a holistic approach, helping my clients to examine all aspects of their lives in order to bring about change. I adapt to the client's starting point, guiding them collaboratively and adding insights as needed. I ask questions to uncover underlying issues and expand the narrative of their life story. I draw from Emotionally Focused Therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, and Narrative Therapy models in my practice.
